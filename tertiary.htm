Cookies

Cookies are files Web browsers place on a computer's hard drive. They are used to help us authenticate clients and provide timesaving shortcuts and preferences. Brandfinity uses a persistent encrypted cookie, if the client requests, to save and retrieve individual authentication information as well as other per-client device preferences. When a visitor follows a link to the Brandfinity’s Web site from an affiliate or promotion, Brandfinity stores information about that affiliate or promotion in a session cookie. Acceptance of these cookies is optional. Brandfinity issues a mandatory session cookie, to each user of the Services only to record encrypted authentication information for the duration of a specific session. If you reject this cookie, access to and usage of the Services will be denied.

Security

These safeguards help prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy and ensure the appropriate use of data.
